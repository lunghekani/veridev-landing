Founders Note Here?

Hello, I am Charnelle and I am a non-technical founder building a tech-based business without a tech cofounder :) . The process of looking for a tech founder has been seemingly never-ending and riddled with frustration. It is difficult to convince a developer of your vision, albeit brilliant, in the early stages. Especially if you don't have a tech product already, It feels like you're asking them to give up their security and join you on the unpredictable journey of growing a startup.

Well, at VeriDev we believe in your world-changing idea so much so that we created a solution that gives you the ability to get your ideas off the ground and offers you the stability as if a tech founder were present. In recognizing that the journey is unpredictable and it takes time to find a tech co-founder; we build with you, not for you. We go the distance because an app is never finished. Along my own journey of building this business, I discovered that the solutions that currently exist do not solve 3 specific problems that are specific to early-stage non-technical startup founders:

  • As a non-technical founder I didn’t know where exactly to find reliable, competent and most importantly, affordable developers
  • there was seemingly this communication barrier between myself (the non-technical party) and the developers (technical party) that led to misunderstandings and made it difficult to managing expectations. Often I walked away unsatisfied with the final product and lastly,
  • I didn't know what the tell-tale signs of a competent and reliable developer were leading to being scammed because I chose the wrong person.

This led to me wanting to build a viable solution that is empathetic to the nuanced issues experienced by non-technical startup founders and solopreneurs. Charnelle xx Connect with me *linkedin profile*
